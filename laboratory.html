Testing and diagnostic services
Laboratory services refer to testing and diagnostic services. These services are essential for monitoring the environment, assessing population health, investigating disease outbreaks, controlling communicable diseases, and ensuring food and water safety. Clinical laboratory services provide objective health information for disease prevention and diagnosis. Clinical Laboratory Science is the health profession that provides laboratory information and services needed for the diagnosis and treatment of disease.

In hospitals and other patient-care settings, laboratory medicine is provided by the Department of Pathology and Medical Laboratory, and generally divided into two sections, each of which will be subdivided into multiple specialty areas.
