Giardiasis

Overview

Giardiasis is an intestinal infection caused by the parasite Giardia lamblia.

Causes

Contaminated water, food, or person-to-person contact.

Symptoms

  • Diarrhea
  • Bloating and gas
  • Weight loss
  • Stomach cramps

Treatment

Medications like metronidazole or tinidazole.

Prevention

Drinking safe water and practicing good hygiene.
