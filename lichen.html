Lichen Planus

Overview

Lichen Planus is an inflammatory condition that affects the skin, hair, nails, and mucous membranes.

Causes

Believed to be related to autoimmune reactions. Triggers may include hepatitis C, medications, or allergens.

Symptoms

  • Itchy, purplish skin bumps
  • White patches in the mouth
  • Hair loss or nail damage

Treatment

Treatment may include corticosteroids, antihistamines, or light therapy.

Prevention

Avoid triggers and practice healthy lifestyle and oral hygiene.
