Medulloblastoma

Overview

Medulloblastoma is a fast-growing brain tumor that originates in the cerebellum, primarily affecting children.

Causes

  • Genetic mutations
  • Inherited cancer syndromes

Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Nausea and vomiting
  • Balance and coordination problems
  • Vision changes

Treatment

Surgery, radiation therapy, and chemotherapy. Targeted therapy may also be considered.

Prevention

  • No known prevention; genetic counseling may help high-risk families
