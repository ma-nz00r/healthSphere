Kallmann Syndrome

Overview

Kallmann Syndrome is a genetic condition characterized by delayed or absent puberty and an impaired sense of smell.

Causes

Caused by gene mutations that affect hormone production and brain development.

Symptoms

  • Delayed puberty
  • Reduced sense of smell
  • Infertility

Treatment

Treatment involves hormone replacement therapy and fertility treatments if needed.

Prevention

Not preventable, but early diagnosis and therapy improve quality of life.
