Lennox-Gastaut Syndrome

Overview

Lennox-Gastaut Syndrome is a severe form of childhood epilepsy with multiple seizure types.

Causes

May be due to brain malformations, infections, or genetic disorders.

Symptoms

  • Frequent seizures
  • Developmental delays
  • Cognitive impairment

Treatment

Includes anti-seizure medications, ketogenic diet, and surgery in some cases.

Prevention

No prevention; early treatment helps reduce complications.
