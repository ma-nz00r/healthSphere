Hyperkinetic Disorder

Overview

Hyperkinetic Disorder is a childhood behavioral disorder characterized by excessive activity, impulsivity, and attention difficulties. It is similar to ADHD.

Causes

  • Genetic predisposition
  • Neurodevelopmental abnormalities
  • Environmental stressors

Symptoms

  • Excessive restlessness
  • Short attention span
  • Impulsive behavior
  • Difficulty following instructions

Treatment

Managed with behavioral therapy, parent training, and sometimes medication (stimulants).

Prevention

No specific prevention, but early diagnosis and support improve outcomes.
