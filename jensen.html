Jensen Syndrome

Overview

Jensen Syndrome is an extremely rare genetic condition affecting skeletal and connective tissue development.

Causes

Linked to genetic mutations that impair bone and tissue growth.

Symptoms

  • Skeletal abnormalities
  • Joint problems
  • Growth delay

Treatment

No specific cure; orthopedic interventions and therapy help manage symptoms.

Prevention

Genetic counseling is recommended for families with history.
