X-Linked Ichthyosis

Overview

A genetic skin disorder causing dry, scaly skin from birth, primarily in males.

Causes

  • Mutations in the STS gene.
  • Inherited in an X-linked recessive manner.

Symptoms

  • Thick, dark, scaly patches on skin.
  • Rough skin texture, especially on trunk and limbs.

Treatment

  • Regular moisturizing and keratolytic creams.
  • Topical retinoids in severe cases.

Prevention

  • Genetic counseling for affected families.
