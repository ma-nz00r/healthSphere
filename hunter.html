Hunter Syndrome

Overview

Hunter Syndrome is a rare inherited genetic disorder that affects the bodyâ€™s ability to break down certain complex sugars.

Causes

Caused by deficiency of the enzyme iduronate sulfatase.

Symptoms

  • Thickened facial features
  • Enlarged liver and spleen
  • Respiratory issues
  • Joint stiffness

Treatment

Treated with enzyme replacement therapy and supportive care.

Prevention

No prevention, but genetic screening is advised for families with history.
