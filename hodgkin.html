Hodgkin Lymphoma

Overview

Hodgkin Lymphoma is a type of cancer that originates in the lymphatic system, which is part of the immune system.

Causes

The exact cause is unknown but linked to genetic mutations and sometimes Epstein-Barr virus.

Symptoms

  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Night sweats
  • Fever
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue

Treatment

Common treatments include chemotherapy, radiation therapy, and stem cell transplantation.

Prevention

No sure prevention, but early diagnosis improves outcomes.
