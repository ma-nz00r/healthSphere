Dr. Ali

Neuro Surgeon

Biography

Dr. Ali is a highly skilled neurosurgeon with over 15 years of experience in diagnosing and treating complex neurological conditions. He is recognized for his dedication, patient-centered care, and advanced surgical techniques that improve patients’ quality of life.

Qualification

  • MBBS – King Edward Medical University

Experience

With more than 15 years of practice, Dr. Ali has performed numerous complex neurosurgical operations including brain tumor resections, spinal surgeries, and trauma management. His expertise ensures safe and successful outcomes for his patients.
