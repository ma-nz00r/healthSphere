Melanoma

Overview

Melanoma is a serious form of skin cancer that develops in the cells (melanocytes) responsible for producing melanin.

Causes

  • Prolonged UV exposure from sun or tanning beds
  • Genetic mutations
  • Family history of melanoma

Symptoms

  • Unusual mole growth
  • Changes in mole shape, size, or color
  • Itching, bleeding, or pain in a mole

Treatment

Surgery, immunotherapy, radiation therapy, and targeted drug therapy depending on stage.

Prevention

  • Use sunscreen with SPF 30+
  • Avoid tanning beds
  • Regular skin checks
