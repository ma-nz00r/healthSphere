Jock Itch

Overview

Jock Itch is a common fungal infection of the groin area, causing itching and redness.

Causes

Caused by fungus (dermatophytes) thriving in warm, moist environments.

Symptoms

  • Red rash in groin
  • Itching and burning
  • Flaky or scaly skin

Treatment

Antifungal creams, powders, and proper hygiene help clear infection.

Prevention

Keep groin dry, wear loose clothing, and avoid sharing towels.
