Insomnia

Overview

Insomnia is a common sleep disorder characterized by difficulty falling or staying asleep.

Causes

Stress, anxiety, poor sleep habits, certain medications, and medical conditions.

Symptoms

  • Difficulty falling asleep
  • Frequent awakenings at night
  • Daytime fatigue
  • Difficulty concentrating

Treatment

Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), sleep hygiene, and medications if needed.

Prevention

Maintain a regular sleep schedule, avoid caffeine before bed, and manage stress.
