Japanese Encephalitis

Overview

Japanese Encephalitis is a viral brain infection spread by mosquitoes, common in parts of Asia.

Causes

Caused by the Japanese encephalitis virus transmitted through mosquito bites.

Symptoms

  • Fever and headache
  • Stiff neck
  • Seizures
  • Confusion and coma

Treatment

No specific cure; supportive care with fluids, seizure control, and intensive care.

Prevention

Vaccination, mosquito control, and protective clothing reduce risk.
