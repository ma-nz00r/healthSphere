Glioma

HealthSphere’s Approach

People with glioma who seek care at HealthSphere will find dedicated specialists who provide expert, personalized care using the latest treatment innovations and technology.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

At HealthSphere, specialists within the Brain Tumor Program work together to provide comprehensive care for people with glioma. Your team may include neuro-oncologists, neurosurgeons, neurologists, oncologists, radiation oncologists, pathologists, and radiologists. This collaborative approach ensures rapid test results, efficient scheduling, and treatment plans that are typically developed within just a few days.

Advanced Diagnosis and Treatment

Experience caring for many patients. HealthSphere specialists treat thousands of adults and children with glioma every year.

Skilled neurosurgeons. Our neurosurgeons perform hundreds of brain tumor surgeries each year, using advanced technologies such as computer-assisted brain surgery, intraoperative MRI, awake brain surgery, and laser techniques.

Leadership in research. HealthSphere scientists and researchers are committed to investigating the causes of gliomas and developing new, more effective treatments.

The HealthSphere Experience

Patients consistently highlight the quality of interactions, attention to detail, and efficiency of their visits. Many describe their care at HealthSphere as unlike anything they have experienced before.

Specialized Diagnosis

Accurate diagnosis is critical for glioma treatment. HealthSphere doctors use the latest imaging technologies — including functional MRI, perfusion MRI, and magnetic resonance spectroscopy — to ensure precise diagnosis and effective treatment planning.

  • Computer-assisted brain surgery
  • Intraoperative MRI
  • Awake brain surgery
  • Laser surgery
