Insulinoma

Overview

Insulinoma is a rare tumor of the pancreas that produces excess insulin, leading to low blood sugar.

Causes

Caused by abnormal growth of pancreatic beta cells.

Symptoms

  • Confusion and dizziness
  • Blurred vision
  • Shaking and sweating
  • Loss of consciousness

Treatment

Prevention

No known prevention, but early detection and treatment prevent complications.
