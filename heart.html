HealthSphere’s Approach

People with heart arrhythmia who seek care at HealthSphere will find dedicated specialists who provide expert, personalized care using the latest treatment innovations and technology.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

At HealthSphere, doctors trained in cardiology, electrophysiology, cardiovascular surgery, and other specialties collaborate as a multidisciplinary team to provide the most effective care for people with arrhythmia and other heart conditions.

Advanced Diagnosis and Treatment

One-stop care. A detailed itinerary for appointments, tests, and procedures allows patients to make the most of their time at HealthSphere. Many tests taken in the morning can often be reviewed the same afternoon.

Innovative diagnosis and treatment. Researchers in HealthSphere’s Cardiovascular Research Center are developing new diagnostic tools and innovative treatments for people with cardiovascular disease.

Cutting-edge research. Experts in the Heart Rhythm Clinic study genetics, causes, diagnostic tests, and treatments for arrhythmia while conducting clinical trials to improve outcomes.
