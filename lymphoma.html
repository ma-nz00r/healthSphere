Follicular Lymphoma Cancer

Overview

Follicular lymphoma is a slow-growing type of non-Hodgkin lymphoma that affects lymph nodes.

Causes

Genetic mutations and immune system dysfunction.

Symptoms

  • Painless swollen lymph nodes
  • Night sweats
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue

Treatment

Radiation, chemotherapy, targeted therapy, or immunotherapy.

Prevention

No guaranteed prevention; healthy lifestyle may reduce cancer risks.
