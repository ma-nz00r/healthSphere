Juvenile Idiopathic Arthritis (JIA)

Overview

JIA is the most common type of arthritis in children, causing persistent joint inflammation.

Causes

Exact cause unknown; believed to be autoimmune with genetic and environmental factors.

Symptoms

  • Joint pain and swelling
  • Stiffness (especially morning)
  • Fatigue

Treatment

Includes NSAIDs, DMARDs, physical therapy to reduce pain and improve mobility.

Prevention

No prevention, but early diagnosis improves outcomes.
