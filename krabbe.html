Krabbe Disease

Overview

Krabbe Disease is a rare inherited disorder affecting the nervous system, causing progressive damage to the brain and spinal cord.

Causes

Caused by deficiency of the GALC enzyme, leading to toxic buildup of certain fats in the nervous system.

Symptoms

  • Developmental delay
  • Muscle weakness and stiffness
  • Seizures
  • Vision and hearing loss

Treatment

No cure; stem cell transplant may help if done early, along with supportive care.

Prevention

Genetic counseling and newborn screening help in early detection.
