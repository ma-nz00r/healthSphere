Kidney Cancer

Overview

Kidney Cancer is a type of cancer that begins in the kidneys, most commonly as renal cell carcinoma.

Causes

Risk factors include smoking, obesity, high blood pressure, and family history.

Symptoms

  • Blood in urine
  • Flank or back pain
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue

Treatment

Treatments include surgery, targeted therapy, immunotherapy, and radiation in advanced cases.

Prevention

Healthy lifestyle choices such as quitting smoking and maintaining healthy weight reduce risk.
