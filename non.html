Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma

Overview

Non-Hodgkin Lymphoma (NHL) is a cancer of the lymphatic system, which helps fight infections in the body.

Causes

The exact cause is unknown but risk factors include weakened immune system, viral infections, age, and exposure to chemicals.

Symptoms

  • Swollen lymph nodes
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fever and night sweats
  • Fatigue
  • Chest pain or coughing

Treatment

Treatment may include chemotherapy, radiation therapy, targeted therapy, and stem cell transplantation.

Prevention

No guaranteed prevention, but maintaining a healthy immune system and avoiding carcinogenic chemicals can help lower risk.
