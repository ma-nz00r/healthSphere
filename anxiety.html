Generalized Anxiety Disorder (GAD)

Overview

Generalized Anxiety Disorder is a mental health condition characterized by persistent and excessive worry about everyday situations.

Causes

Genetics, brain chemistry, trauma, and chronic stress.

Symptoms

  • Excessive worry
  • Restlessness
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Insomnia

Treatment

Cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT), antidepressants, and lifestyle changes.

Prevention

Stress management, exercise, and mindfulness practices.
