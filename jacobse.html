Jacobsen Syndrome

Overview

Jacobsen Syndrome is a rare genetic disorder caused by deletion of chromosome 11 material.

Causes

Results from missing genetic material on chromosome 11.

Symptoms

  • Developmental delay
  • Bleeding disorders
  • Heart defects
  • Distinct facial features

Treatment

No cure; treatment is symptom-based with therapies and surgery if needed.

Prevention

Genetic counseling can help families understand risks.
