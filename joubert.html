Joubert Syndrome

Overview

Joubert Syndrome is a rare genetic disorder affecting brain development, especially the cerebellum.

Causes

Caused by mutations in multiple genes linked to brain structure.

Symptoms

  • Poor muscle tone
  • Abnormal eye movements
  • Developmental delays
  • Breathing irregularities

Treatment

No cure; treatment includes physical therapy, speech therapy, and supportive care.

Prevention

Genetic testing and counseling can help families plan.
