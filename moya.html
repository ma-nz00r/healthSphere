Moyamoya Disease

Overview

Moyamoya disease is a rare condition in which the arteries at the base of the brain are narrowed or blocked, leading to stroke risk.

Causes

  • Genetic mutations
  • Associated with other conditions such as Down syndrome and sickle cell disease

Symptoms

  • Stroke or transient ischemic attacks
  • Headaches
  • Seizures
  • Weakness or numbness

Treatment

Surgical revascularization is the primary treatment to restore blood flow. Medications may help prevent clots.

Prevention

  • No known prevention; early diagnosis and surgery reduce risks
