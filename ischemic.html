Ischemic Stroke

Overview

Ischemic Stroke occurs when a blood clot blocks blood flow to the brain, causing tissue damage.

Causes

Mainly due to blood clots, atherosclerosis, or heart conditions like atrial fibrillation.

Symptoms

  • Sudden weakness on one side of body
  • Slurred speech
  • Vision problems
  • Severe headache

Treatment

Immediate treatment with clot-busting drugs (tPA), surgery, and rehabilitation therapy.

Prevention

Healthy lifestyle, control of blood pressure, cholesterol, and diabetes.
