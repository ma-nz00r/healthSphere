Influenza

Overview

Influenza (flu) is a viral respiratory infection that spreads easily through droplets.

Causes

Caused by influenza A or B viruses, spread by coughing, sneezing, or contact with contaminated surfaces.

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Cough and sore throat
  • Muscle aches
  • Fatigue
  • Headaches

Treatment

Rest, hydration, antiviral medications (oseltamivir), and symptom relief medications.

Prevention

Annual flu vaccine, hand hygiene, and avoiding close contact with sick people.
