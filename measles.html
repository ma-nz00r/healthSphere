Measles

Overview

Measles is a highly contagious viral disease that primarily affects children but can occur at any age.

Causes

  • Caused by the measles virus
  • Spread through coughs, sneezes, and direct contact

Symptoms

  • High fever
  • Cough, runny nose, and red eyes
  • Characteristic red rash
  • Koplik spots inside the mouth

Treatment

No specific antiviral treatment. Supportive care includes hydration, fever control, and vitamin A supplementation.

Prevention

  • MMR vaccine (Measles, Mumps, Rubella)
  • Good hygiene and avoiding close contact with infected individuals
