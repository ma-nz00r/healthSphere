Gonorrhea

Overview

Gonorrhea is a sexually transmitted infection (STI) caused by the bacterium Neisseria gonorrhoeae.

Causes

Unprotected sexual contact with an infected person.

Symptoms

  • Painful urination
  • Abnormal discharge
  • Pelvic pain
  • Sometimes asymptomatic

Treatment

Antibiotics such as ceftriaxone and azithromycin.

Prevention

Safe sex practices and regular STI testing.
