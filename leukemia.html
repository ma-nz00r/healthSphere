Leukemia

Overview

Leukemia is a cancer of blood-forming tissues, affecting white blood cells.

Causes

Exact cause unknown; risks include radiation, genetics, and smoking.

Symptoms

  • Frequent infections
  • Easy bruising/bleeding
  • Fatigue
  • Swollen lymph nodes

Treatment

Includes chemotherapy, radiation therapy, targeted therapy, and stem cell transplant.

Prevention

No guaranteed prevention, but avoiding radiation and chemicals reduces risk.
