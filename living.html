Living-Donor Transplant

HealthSphere Approach

HealthSphere has one of the most comprehensive transplant practices. Our transplant doctors, surgeons, and staff at multiple HealthSphere campuses have extensive experience with living-donor kidney and liver transplantation.

Team approach. HealthSphere's integrated teams of surgeons, doctors, transplant nurses, pharmacists, and other specialists work together to provide care — from evaluation through post-surgery recovery. Donors undergo blood tests, detailed imaging, physical and psychological evaluations to ensure they meet safe donation criteria.

Coordinated care. Having subspecialized expertise in one place means test results are available quickly, appointments are well-coordinated, and your case is reviewed by multiple experts working together for the best outcome.

Surgical expertise. HealthSphere surgeons perform advanced minimally invasive surgeries such as laparoscopic donor nephrectomy, reducing pain and recovery time compared to open surgery.

Research. HealthSphere researchers study the long-term health of donors and recipients, offering access to ongoing clinical trials and innovative treatments.

Bringing Research Advances to Patient Care

Advanced treatment and technology. HealthSphere surgeons specialize in all forms of living-donor transplantation, including minimally invasive techniques, paired-organ donation, HIV-positive organ donation, and other complex cases.

Innovation and research. Our researchers are leading efforts to improve every stage of transplantation, reduce risks, and enhance long-term patient outcomes. The HealthSphere Transplant Center supports numerous research studies focused on donor and recipient care.
