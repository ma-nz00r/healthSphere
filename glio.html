Glioblastoma

Overview

Glioblastoma is an aggressive type of brain cancer that develops from glial cells.

Causes

Genetic mutations, radiation exposure, and family history may increase risk.

Symptoms

  • Headaches
  • Seizures
  • Memory loss
  • Speech or vision problems

Treatment

Surgery, radiation therapy, chemotherapy, and targeted drug therapy.

Prevention

No known prevention, but healthy lifestyle may reduce cancer risks.
