HealthSphere’s Approach

HealthSphere experts have extensive experience using heart valve procedures to treat a wide range of conditions. We offer advanced treatment options for adults and children, including autologous procedures (using a patient’s own cells), allogeneic procedures (from a donor), and innovative techniques for complex cases.

Patients at HealthSphere receive comprehensive, compassionate, and personalized attention from healthcare providers and staff dedicated to meeting their exact needs. In addition, support groups and patient education programs are available to guide families throughout the care journey.

Your HealthSphere Care Team

At HealthSphere, cardiologists, cardiovascular surgeons, and heart valve disease specialists work together to provide coordinated care. This collaborative approach allows your team to evaluate and develop a treatment plan quickly, often within 2–3 days.

The HealthSphere Experience

Our specialists have extensive experience performing heart valve surgeries for adults and children with a variety of conditions. Since the beginning of our program, thousands of successful procedures have been performed, ensuring patient safety and long-term outcomes.

Advanced Diagnosis and Treatment

HealthSphere offers advanced diagnostic tests and treatments for heart valve disease, including aortic, mitral, pulmonary, and tricuspid valve disorders. Our care team uses imaging technologies such as echocardiography, 3D echocardiography, and transesophageal echocardiography to ensure accurate diagnosis and tailored treatment plans.
