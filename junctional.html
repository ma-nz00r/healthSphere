Junctional Epidermolysis Bullosa (JEB)

Overview

JEB is a severe genetic skin disorder where fragile skin blisters easily.

Causes

Caused by gene mutations affecting proteins that anchor skin layers together.

Symptoms

  • Widespread skin blisters
  • Fragile nails
  • Difficulty feeding
  • Scarring

Treatment

No cure; wound care, nutritional support, and infection prevention are essential.

Prevention

Genetic counseling helps at-risk families. Prenatal testing is possible.
