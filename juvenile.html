Juvenile Myelomonocytic Leukemia (JMML)

Overview

JMML is a rare childhood leukemia affecting blood-forming cells in bone marrow.

Causes

Caused by gene mutations that lead to uncontrolled growth of blood cells.

Symptoms

  • Frequent infections
  • Enlarged spleen
  • Pale skin
  • Easy bruising

Treatment

The main treatment is bone marrow transplant. Supportive care helps manage symptoms.

Prevention

No prevention; early diagnosis and transplant improve outcomes.
