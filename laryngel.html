Laryngeal Cancer

Overview

Laryngeal Cancer is cancer that develops in the voice box (larynx).

Causes

Risk factors include smoking, alcohol consumption, and HPV infection.

Symptoms

  • Hoarseness
  • Difficulty swallowing
  • Sore throat
  • Neck lump

Treatment

Treatment options include surgery, radiation, chemotherapy, and targeted therapy.

Prevention

Avoid smoking, limit alcohol, and practice safe lifestyle habits.
